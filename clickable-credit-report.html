3 High-Impact Items to Watch

Hover the report items on the left. Then use the guidance below to build your action plan.

  • Missed Payment
    A late payment can be one of the biggest score hits and may affect approvals.
    Fix: autopay + never repeat
  • Collection Account
    Collections can raise red flags for lenders and reduce loan options.
    Fix: verify + resolve or dispute
  • High Utilization
    Even with on-time payments, high utilization can pull scores down.
    Fix: keep under 30% (aim under 10%)
